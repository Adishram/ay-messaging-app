A&Y

Privacy Policy

1. The Short Version

We do not collect, store, or have access to your messages, files, contacts, or identity. By design, it is technologically impossible for us to do so.

2. How A&Y Works

A&Y is a peer-to-peer (P2P) application. Your identity is a cryptographic keypair generated locally on your device. When you send a message, it travels directly from your computer to the recipient's computer via WebRTC.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Account Data: We do not ask for or store your name, email, or phone number.
  • Message Content: All communications are end-to-end encrypted locally using AES-256-GCM. We never touch your plaintext data.
  • Contacts & Metadata: Your contact list and conversation history are stored entirely locally on your machine using IndexedDB.

4. The Signaling Server

To establish a P2P connection, A&Y utilizes a minimal signaling relay. This server temporarily routes encrypted WebRTC handshake data (SDP offers and ICE candidates) so devices can find each other over the internet. This relay operates entirely in-memory, logs no payload data, and serves solely as an ephemeral routing mechanism.

5. Analytics & Telemetry

We do not track you. The A&Y client contains zero analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry modules.
